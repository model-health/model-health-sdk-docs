## Navigating the 3D Musculoskeletal Model

Once generated, the 3D musculoskeletal model can be explored directly in the web interface. Here's how to navigate it:

![3D model viewer](/img/docs/web-app-tips/image.png)

**Zooming In and Out**

Use your mouse scroll wheel to zoom in and out smoothly. Scrolling forward brings you closer; scrolling backward moves you away.

**Rotating the 3D Model**

Click and hold the **left mouse button**, then move your mouse in any direction to rotate the model freely. Useful for examining movement from various viewpoints, including transverse views.

**Translating (Panning) the Model**

Click and hold the **right mouse button**, then move your mouse to pan the model without changing zoom or rotation.

## Add Your Logo

Under your profile, tap **"Add logo"** to display your logo in the web application and include it in PDF reports generated from advanced analyses.

## Share Sessions

On the session page, tap **"Share session publicly"** to generate a shareable link. Anyone with the link can view the session data.

## Collect Data from Multiple Subjects in a Row

Tap **"New session, same setup"** on the session page to skip camera calibration after the first subject. This creates a new session and takes you directly to the subject information entry page. Repeat as needed — cameras only need to be calibrated once.
