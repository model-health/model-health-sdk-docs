Welcome to the Model Health documentation.

## What is Model Health?

Model Health is your fastest way to lab-grade movement analysis. It turns standard iOS devices into a portable biomechanics lab — no marker suits, no specialist cameras, no controlled environment required.

Point two iPhones at a subject, record the movement, and get back 3D kinematics, instant metrics, and shareable reports in minutes. The system is built on research-grade biomechanical models validated against lab standards, making it suitable for rehabilitation, performance, and research settings alike.

**How it works:**
1. **Record** — capture movement through the iOS app using 2 or more iPhones
2. **Reconstruct** — automatic 3D body reconstruction and kinematics processing in the cloud
3. **Analyze** — instant metrics for 12 supported movement assessments
4. **Review** — visualize results, track progress over time, and export PDF reports

**Supported movement assessments include:** countermovement jump, drop vertical jump, hop test, squat exercise, sit-to-stand transfer, overground walking and running, treadmill walking and running, cutting maneuver, 5-0-5 test, and custom activity.
