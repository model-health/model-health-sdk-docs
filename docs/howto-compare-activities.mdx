import React from 'react';

The **Replay** view lets you overlay multiple recordings of the same activity type side by side — useful for tracking progress over time or comparing left vs. right trials.

### Step 1. Open a subject and select a first activity

Navigate to a subject and open any completed activity. In the **Activities** panel on the left, click a trial to load it into the 3D replay view. The skeleton and joint angle graph will update to reflect that recording.

<ZoomableImage
  src="/img/docs/howto-compare-activities/howto-compare-activities-single.png"
  alt="Select a first activity to compare"
  caption="Click any completed activity in the left panel to load it into the Replay view."
/>

### Step 2. Add more activities to the comparison

With the first activity loaded, click additional trials in the **Activities** panel to add them to the overlay. Each activity is assigned a distinct color — the 3D skeletons and the joint angle curves are all rendered simultaneously, making differences in movement pattern immediately visible.
